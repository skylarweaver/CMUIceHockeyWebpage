Carnegie Mellon Ice Hockey

OUR TEAM



There is more to Carnegie Mellon than just academics; there is ice hockey. Founded in 1912, the CMU Ice Hockey Team continues its legacy to this day. Our home rink is Shady Side Academy's beautiful McKnight Arena located just twenty minutes away from campus. We compete in the NCHA Southeastern Conference where we not only play local teams, but out-of-state teams as well. During the 2013-2014 season, we made it to the playoffs for the fifth year in a row and even received a bid to compete in Nationals. In addition to regular season play, we compete in one or two tournaments each year to change up our competition. We generally practice once a week (Wednesday nights) and have roughly one game a week (usually on weekends). This gives us the perfect amount of time to focus on academics at CMU, and also allows us a well deserved break to go enjoy the best sport in the world. As a coed club sport, we have a low barrier to entry and are always looking for new players of all abilities. If you are interested at all or want more information, please feel free to contact us!
